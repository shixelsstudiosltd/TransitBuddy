Sure you can! The best part about TransitBuddy is that you are not bound to just one suitcase we will help you ship multiple items and get them all to you within your delivery timeline.

How do you calculate shipment cost?

We use our one of a kind shipment algroithim to calculate a shipment price based on criterias such as size, weight, and shipment speed to provide you with a fair and comparitevly cheaper alternative for SUPER FAST international "shipping".

Can I ransit multiple items?

Ofcourse! Fill your baggage to your hearts content, the more items you ransit, the more money you make!

Do I have to deliver the items I ransit myself?

Not at all! One of our TransitBuddy couriers will meet you at your destination airport and intercept all Transited items for delivery.
